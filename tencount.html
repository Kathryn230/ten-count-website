The Film

TEN COUNT is an inspirational 90 min documentary that explores mental health and the redemptive power of sport.

Starring Dwayne 'The Rock" Johnson and a host of other world-renowned athletes, Ten Count tries to de-stigmatise mental illness and charts the incredible comeback of ex-Premier league soccer player turned boxer Leon McKenzie and his epic, award-winning last ever professional fight.

Directed by BAFTA long-listed filmmaker Nathanael Wiseman and produced by award-winning film and TV production company Redeeming Features, the film will be released to worldwide audiences in early 2023.

Leon McKenzie is an ambassador for the UK mental health charities CALM and MIND and appears regularly on TALK SPORT as co-host alongside cricketing legend Stephen Harmison on their series ‘AFTER THE LIGHTS GO OUT’.

Leon has given speeches to organizations around the world about mental health and has appeared on BBC, ITV and Sky and been featured in publications around the globe.

Nathanael Wiseman is a BAFTA long-listed actor/writer/director who has interviewed a plethora of athletes and mental health professionals all over the world and has appeared on screen countless times most notably as the lead role in the feature films LONDON’S FINEST and HARD TIDE.
